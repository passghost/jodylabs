Sounds of the Appalachian

Experience the natural soundscape of the Appalachian region—frogs calling from misty ponds, gentle rain on leaves, crickets singing at dusk, and owls echoing through the night. These videos capture the wild beauty and peaceful atmosphere of Appalachian nature.

Volume 1
Listen to the chorus of frogs echoing across Appalachian ponds and wetlands, a signature sound of spring and summer nights.
Volume 2
Experience the gentle and soothing sound of rain falling on Appalachian leaves, creating a peaceful natural ambiance.
Volume 3
Hear the rhythmic chirping of crickets as dusk settles over the Appalachian hills, a calming soundscape for evening relaxation.
Volume 4
Enjoy the haunting calls of owls echoing through Appalachian forests, adding mystery and beauty to the nighttime landscape.
Volume 5
Relax to the gentle babble of mountain streams winding through the Appalachian valleys, a refreshing and tranquil sound.
Volume 6
Immerse yourself in the full soundscape of Appalachian forests—birds, wind, insects, and distant wildlife—capturing the region's natural spirit.
